THE MENU

At Flying Dove, we use the finest ingredients picked locally from local California farms delivered fresh in the morning ready for our customers. The chef spares no expenses with the best meats and truffles imported from Italy and France

We value every meal we make and make sure that each meal is a memorable experience. To accommodate your meal you will also have amazing jazz music played by local California bands such as Tommy Jones
